Welcome to Movember

Movember is a global campaign started in 2003 in Australia and arrived in Brazil in 2008. Its goal is to raise awareness about men's health, especially prostate cancer, which affects around 65,000 men per year in Brazil (INCA 2023). It also addresses topics such as depression, mental health, and cardiovascular disease prevention. Use this site to learn and take care of your health!
