The Importance of Movember

Movember was created to alert men about the need to take care of their health, especially prostate cancer, which is the second most common type among men in Brazil (INCA). The campaign encourages preventive exams, reducing avoidable deaths – about 15,000 men die each year due to this disease (INCA 2023). It also promotes awareness about mental health, depression, and other male health issues, breaking cultural taboos that prevent men from seeking medical help.

Why is it important?

  • Reduces stigma: Many men avoid exams out of shame, but the campaign normalizes it.
  • Increases early diagnoses: 90% of cases are curable if detected early (WHO).
  • Educates on healthy habits: Prevents not only cancer but also heart disease and diabetes.
  • Global impact: In 2022, the campaign reached millions in over 50 countries, saving lives.
